Learn Quran Recitation with Expert Teachers!

Our experienced teachers will guide you in mastering the art of Quran recitation. From correct pronunciation to beautiful recitation, we focus on helping you develop a strong connection with the words of Allah.



Courses We Offer


Noorani Qaida Course

Our "Noorani Qaida Course" is the first step for beginners. Through this course, you or your children can learn the basics of Quranic recitation. Our highly-qualified Quran tutors will guide you in understanding Arabic letters, their pronunciation, and joining them to form words. This course sets the foundation for fluent Quranic reading.

Quran Reading Course

Our "Quran Reading Course" focuses on developing proficiency in reading the Quran with understanding. You will learn to navigate the Arabic text, recognize words, and comprehend their meanings. Our tutors will help you improve fluency and comprehension, enhancing your ability to connect with the message of the Quran.

Tajweed Course

Our comprehensive "Tajweed Course" focuses on the rules and principles of Quranic recitation. You will learn proper pronunciation, articulation, and intonation while reciting the Quran. Our experienced tutors will help you master the correct application of Tajweed rules, enabling you to recite the Quran with clarity and beauty.

Quran Memorization course

Our "Quran Memorization Course" aims to help students memorize the Quran. With the guidance of our qualified tutors, you will follow a structured memorization plan, starting from shorter Surahs (chapters) and gradually progressing to longer ones. Our tutors provide effective memorization techniques and regular assessments to ensure steady progress.


Quran Tafseer Course

Discover the profound meanings of the Quran with our Tafsir Courses at Al Madina Quranic Acadmey. Explore historical context, linguistic intricacies, and practical relevance. Gain insights into verses and Surahs, uncovering themes, wisdom, and practical applications. Engage in advanced Tafsir studies, developing critical thinking skills and analyzing diverse perspectives. Enroll today for a transformative journey of Quranic understanding and spiritual enlightenment.

Prayer and Duaa Lessons

Our Prayer and Du'a Lessons build a strong foundation in Islamic practices. With qualified tutors, we provide comprehensive Quranic recitation instruction, starting from Arabic basics. Learn proper techniques and positions for Salah, while understanding the significance of du'a. Join us worldwide to deepen your connection with Allah. Embark on a transformative journey with us.
